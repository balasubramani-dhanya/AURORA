About Us

OUR HERITAGE

Crafting Time Since 1924

Founded in 1924, AURORA has been at the forefront of horological excellence for a century. What began as a small workshop in Switzerland has evolved into a global symbol of luxury and precision.

Every AURORA timepiece is meticulously assembled by master watchmakers, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation.

WHO WE ARE

Timeless Craftsmanship

At AURORA, we are more than watchmakers — we are guardians of time. With a heritage rooted in Swiss craftsmanship, our mission is to create timepieces that blend precision engineering with timeless elegance.

Each watch is carefully designed and assembled by skilled artisans, ensuring exceptional quality and performance. Our dedication to detail and innovation has made AURORA a trusted name in luxury timepieces worldwide.

Design

Great design is where every watch begins. We focus on clean details, balanced shapes, and timeless style so each piece feels elegant today and years from now.

Innovation

We constantly explore new ideas and technologies to improve precision and performance, while still respecting the traditions of fine watchmaking.

Journey

Our journey is driven by passion and craftsmanship. Every step we take is about creating watches that celebrate meaningful moments and lasting memories.
